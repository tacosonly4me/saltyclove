About Us

Located in the quaint neighborhood of Northeast Minneapolis, The Salty Clove is a farm-to-table dining experience designed to bring that small farm feel to the city. We offer a seasonal menu featuring fresh foods from our urban farm in addition to a few farms located in the Greater-Minneapolis area.

You can expect deliciously fresh meals prepared for meat-eaters and vegheads alike. If you don't see something on the menu for you, ask us and we are more than happy to accomodate. We hope that our little corner of Northeast can be a favorite for everyone!
